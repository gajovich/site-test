What distinguishes us as a first class renovation company in Mallorca

We are a general contractor based in Mallorca and specialise in complete renovations and refurbishments. Our versatility, reliability and precision in execution have enabled us, as a renovation company, to achieve the high standards that our clients appreciate.

Our demanding clients include national and international private owners, hotels and businesses. Together with our reliable network and our passionate employees, it is our goal to carry out every project in the highest quality and to the absolute satisfaction of our customers. We are proud of the projects we have carried out in all regions of Mallorca and will be happy to provide references on request.
