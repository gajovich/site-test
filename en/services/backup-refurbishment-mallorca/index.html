Refurbishment on Mallorca

As a competent specialist for refurbishments on Mallorca, we are at your side right from the start with your refurbishment project. Whether reconstruction, refurbishments or modernizations. Our customers include private owners, architects, hotel owners, business owners and gastronomes. ENVIVIR takes over all kinds of refurbishments, windows and doors assembly, tiles and floor laying of all kinds and natural stone terraces, dry construction work, interior work, plastering and filling work, etc.

With us, you get all services from a single provider and including a personal, English-speaking contact - from free initial visit, consultation and quotation preparation to detailed planning of your refurbishment project, material supply and execution of all work.

We will soon let your property shine in new splendour!

Full refurbishments from a single provider

With the refurbishment of your property on Mallorca you can lean back carefree, because we take care of everything for you from the beginning. Only products and materials, which are subject to your individual taste, are to be selected by you. Local competence in refurbishment on Mallorca

As a company based in Mallorca, our specialised employees have many years of experience with local construction methods and materials. We are also familiar with the local climatic conditions and we specialise in remedying and preventing damage caused by the weather and construction methods.

Our network is well established with contacts to well-known national and international manufacturers and to specialized producers and suppliers on the Balearic Islands and on the Spanish mainland. We work exclusively with certified specialists at a very high level.

Whether you want to refurbish your business, your office, your villa, your house, your apartment or studio or parts of it - we will master any refurbishment project and guarantee a final result to your complete satisfaction.
