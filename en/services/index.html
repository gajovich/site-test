Individual and complete solutions for building projects in Mallorca

Your project is much more than a property. It is the place of the future for great ideas, community and growth. So you don't need a construction company, you need a partner.

For you, as a client, we offer comprehensive services and individual solutions for each project size and for each property. We take care of the complete planning and execution of the work at the customer's request. From the approval to the delivery of the keys.

Of course, you can order a small project from us, such as a repair or modernization of your bathroom.

general contractor

As general contractor for construction works on Mallorca we coordinate all disciplines at your request.

project management

We offer the management of construction projects and building work from the initial planning to completion.

Refurbishment

We carry out complete and comprehensive refurbishments with professionals who have all the necessary qualifications.

Remediation

Whether mould infestation or damage to roofs, facades or concrete - we eliminate the causes and remediate professionally.

interior design

We plan and design your project with absolute stylistic confidence and following the latest interior design trends.

bathroom renovations

We design and build your new bathroom, from new installations to the assembly of sanitary facilities.

kitchen renovations

We build your new kitchen, from consultation and conception to installation, delivery and assembly.

pool construction

Your dream is an infinity pool, a natural pool or a micro-cement pool? We build all types of swimming pools.

Our one-stop service - maximum comfort for you as a customer

We want you to feel well advised and in good hands right from the start - that's why we offer you a complete one-stop service. From the free initial consultation and preparation of an offer to your personal, English-speaking contact person and project manager to the execution of all work. We accompany you in all phases of your building project and discuss exactly all steps under consideration of the economic efficiency and the individual building situation.

We would be pleased to visit you on site to provide you with a free and non-binding offer.
